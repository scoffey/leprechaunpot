How to play

  1. Make a prediction: Fill in the results of all 64 matches. The World Cup tournament consists of 2 stages:

    • Group stage, a round-robin tournament where each team plays every other team in its four-team group. Both the winner and runner-up from each group advance to the knockout stage.

    • Knockout stage, 4 series of single-elimination games between the 16 qualifying teams from the group stage.

      IMPORTANT: REGARDING TIES: This app applies the official FIFA rules. (If group play does not break a tie, drawing of lots will be simulated.) In case of a tie-break by penalty shootout during the knockout stage, an extra point in the original score will be considered for the winner.

  2. Challenge your friends: Invite your friends to make predictions so you can compare results.

  3. Check out the leaderboard: Find out who makes the best World Cup prediction according to the following scoring system:

    • 2 points for each exact score guessed in the group stage
    • 3 points for each exact score guessed in the knockout stage
    • 1 point for each match result guessed in the group stage (who wins or if there is a tie only)
    • 2 points for each group winner or runner-up guessed (out of 16)
    • 3 points for each quarter-finalist guessed (out of 8)
    • 4 points for each semi-finalist guessed (out of 4)
    • 5 points for each finalist guessed (out of 2)
    • 6 points for the 3rd place guess
    • 7 points for the World Cup winner guess
